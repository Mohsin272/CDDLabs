Exercise Six: Producer-Consumer  1.0
This lab demonstrates the producer and consumer problem using a finite buffer. The producer generates random letters from 'a' to 'z' and adds it to a thread safe buffer. Once the buffer limit is reached a 'X' is added. The consumer takes these chracters and counts how many consumed until a 'X' is found. Author - Mohsin Tahir
